About Us!

Gems Little Hands is an educational institution that focuses on the early developmental years of children before they enter formal schooling. Pre-primary schools cater to children typically between the ages of 3 to 5 years old. It has been an acive institute since 2016.
The main objective of our school is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can develop their cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills. These schools play a crucial role in preparing children for the transition to primary education by laying the foundation for future learning.

Our Director

The Director of our esteemed Pre-Primary School, Mrs.Ritu Gupta, is passionate and dedicated educator with a Bachelor of Science degree in Early Childhood Education.
She is also a notable member of "Inner-Wheel Club".
With her several years of experience in the field, she brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise in nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning in the early stages of development.
As the Director, her primary goal is to create a warm, inclusive, and stimulating environment where every child feels safe, valued, and inspired to explore their natural curiosity. She believes in providing a solid foundation for their educational journey through a well-rounded approach that combines academic excellence, social-emotional development, and creative expression.
